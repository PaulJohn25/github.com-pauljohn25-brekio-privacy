Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026

Overview

Brekio is a break timer app that helps you manage your break time. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Data Collection

Brekio does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. We have no access to any information on your device.

Data Storage

All data — including your break settings, leave time, and preferences — is stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Notifications

Brekio uses local scheduled notifications to remind you when your break is ending. These notifications are generated entirely on your device and do not involve any external servers or services.

Third-Party Services

Brekio does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.
